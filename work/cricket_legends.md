### Overview
A high-stakes cricket campaign built around historical match moments. Players jump into the action mid-game with the objective to change or uphold the historical result.

### Key Features
*   **Historical Moments**: Replay iconic matches from cricket history.
*   **Mid-Game Entry**: Immediate action by starting in critical overs.
*   **Dynamic Difficulty**: Adapts to player skill to maintain tension.
