--- layout: default copy title: Sell Your Head ---

This is a Tabletop game that aims to bring people together. Using Game theory principles that intrinsically motivate players to share information, co-operate or deceit each other. This prototype is completely playable with some guaranteed conflicts and resolution amoung a group of 3.


Devolepment period - 6 weeks.
