A game on Game Theory

Hey there!

Sell your head is a table-top card game. It is built based on the principles of game theory, considering the persona's that play the game. It allows players to exploit each other and also face its concequences.

The game is currently in its 3rd prototype and very much playable. Check it out and let me know. We can talk about it over a game of Sell your head.
