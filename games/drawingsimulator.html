Dear designer

Yea. You. As our new lead of Graphics department you are going to be making posters for every billboard in the city. We will give you the end product. You just need draw it. We need an exact copy. Any changes you make will come with heavy penalty.

Unless those changes are ordered by the government.

Welcome to Drawing Simulator

Say you great at your work. Just to end up on a desk job and not use any of your skills. Following instructions to the dot and changing things around for the government, but in reality you have the potential to change the entire nation's views. You could start a revolution. Would you do it???

Here is a game I made out of a short story I wrote.
